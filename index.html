How to apply

What Is The Process?

Initial Interest

Once you express interest in relocating to the UK, simply fill our application form and submit it along with the required documents after payment has been made:


  • 1. Curriculum Vitae (CV) showcasing your Care/Support or Construction background.
  • 2. Clear, color copy of your passport data page with photographs.
  • 3. A guarantor.
  • 4. Payment of N250,000 non-refundable processing fee

Approval

Upon approval, you will proceed to the next step by paying for your Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS). Before your interview, you are required to pay £15,000. The CoS is essential for obtaining your visa.

Visa Application

We will provide you with the necessary documentation, including your CoS, for your visa application. You will need to submit:


  • 1. Clear, color copy of your passport.
  • 2. Address for your UK application.
  • 3. Police clearance (to be provided later).
  • 4. UKVI IELTS (if available, optional).
  • 5. TB test results.
  • 6. Proof of COVID-19 vaccination.
  • 7. Bank statement (balance not crucial).

Contract and Additional Forms

Once we receive your passport copy and UK address, we will send you the contract. You will then need to complete:


  • 1. Application form.
  • 2. Health declaration.
  • 3. Two professional references with company stamps, along with a covering letter on letterhead.
  • 4. One character reference.
